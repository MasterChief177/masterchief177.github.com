Modular Ad-Block Catalog for Vivaldi

In this project, I created a structured and modular GitHub repository (a 'catalog') that provides a curated collection of the most effective and reliable ad-blocker lists for the Vivaldi browser. My main task was the research and organization of these lists to offer Vivaldi users a granularly customizable and highly flexible solution, which is available as an open-source resource.

The clear structure of the project allows users to compile individual protection profiles (e.g. only malware and tracking, or complete blocking of advertising), which maximizes control over the browsing experience. This central resource significantly simplifies configuration and guarantees a safe, fast and personalized browsing experience.

Technical Skills

  • Research and analysis skills: Conducting in-depth research into the most effective and reliable ad-blocker lists on the market. Analyzing tracking mechanisms and their granularity to provide a highly flexible solution.
  • Data Curation & Information Management: Structured selection, organization and maintenance of a modular collection of block lists to provide Vivaldi users with a secure and individual protection profile.
  • Version Control (Git/GitHub): Establishment of a GitHub repository as an open-source resource for transparent management of changes, ensuring the structure and enabling collaborative further development.
